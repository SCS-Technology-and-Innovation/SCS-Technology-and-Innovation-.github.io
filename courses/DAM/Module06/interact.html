Stock-market, continued

  • Find a company the stock of which you'd like to "fake trade".
  • Like in the previous module, download the historical data from Yahoo Finance.
  • Select and then process the data below.
  • Press space bar to advance to the next day.
  • Your current day will always be on the right side of the plot. Zoom out if you cannot see the right extreme of the black box.
  • Press b to buy.
  • Press s to sell.
  • Press r to start over.
  • Transactions are all-or-nothing, for simplicity, and use the random price from the day's range.
  • Unrealistically, we assume your transaction to have no effect on the stock prices.
