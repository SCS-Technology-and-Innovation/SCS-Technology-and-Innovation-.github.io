Recap

This module follows up on everything we have seen in the course thus far with the question: when and how should we be making use of this technology? Being able to put together a piece of code that spits out some outputs based on data we shoved into it does not imply that it would necessarily be a good or even a neutral thing to do so. Real harm can be done, and has been done, with irresponsible use of technology.
