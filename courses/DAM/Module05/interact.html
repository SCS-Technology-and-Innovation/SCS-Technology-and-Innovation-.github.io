Stock-market data

  • Find a company the stock price of which you'd like to analyze.
  • Go to Yahoo Finance.
  • Locate and examine the data for that particular stock.
    For example, the page for Pharmala Biotech Holdings Inc. (MDMA.CN) is at ca.finance.yahoo.com/quote/MDMA.CN
  • Click on the option Historical Data on the tabs right underneath the current price.
  • This takes you to a raw-data page
    For example, MDMA.CN/history?p=MDMA.CN
  • From the raw-data page, select the time period and the frequency of your interest.
  • Press the Apply button.
  • Click on Download (right underneath the button).
  • On your computer, there is now a .csv file.
    (Most likely in your customary Downloads folder unless you specified another location)
  • Repeat for at least one more stock, with the same period and frequency.
