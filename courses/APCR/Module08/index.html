Readings

Any online resource for LaTeX is fine. The McGill Library contains various ebooks that may be helpful:

  • Kottwitz: LaTeX beginnger's guide https://mcgill.on.worldcat.org/oclc/727050623
  • Gratzer: Practical LaTeX https://mcgill.on.worldcat.org/oclc/889578689
  • Kottwitz: LaTeX graphics with TikZ https://mcgill.on.worldcat.org/oclc/1381073590
  • Öschner & Öschner: Advanced LaTeX in academia https://mcgill.on.worldcat.org/oclc/1294345511

Tools

You can either install LaTeX on your computer by downloading a software such as TeX Live or use LaTeX in Overleaf. Either option works just as well and the former does not require you to create any online accounts.
