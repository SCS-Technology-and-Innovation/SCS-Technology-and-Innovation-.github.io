Hands-on option

Basic stage for the hands-on option

Interacting with the model and solution method studied in class, examine systematically how the following factors appear to affect the percentage of appointments that are successfully scheduled:

  1. Number of patients
  2. Average number of appointments per patient
  3. Probability of doctor availability
  4. Probability of equipment availabiility

Explain first the methodology you have designed for your systematic examination, then report your results, and conclude with an interpretation of the results.

In-depth stage for the hands-on option

What other factors are there that also appear to affect the percentage of appointments scheduled? Draft some guidelines on how one could attempt to map out such dependencies between adjustable parameters and the characteristics of the resulting solution.

Apply your guidelines to this scenario and report your findings. Include both the guidelines and the findings in clear writing in your response.
