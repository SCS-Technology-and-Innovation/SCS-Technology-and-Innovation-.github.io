Website visits

In order to use this tool with real data, you need to have on your computer the monthly data exports from the central dashboard, named jan23.csv (month with three lowercase letters, year with two digits). No data is ever uploaded anywhere or obtained from elsewhere, all processing is done locally on your own computer based on the files you have.

The horizontal axis of the plot shows the time (month / year). The curve shown by default are the total visits. You can hide that and/or add curves for selections of pages of your choice and pick a color for each.
