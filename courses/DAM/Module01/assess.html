Hands-on option

Basic stage for the hands-on option

Experiment with the simulation to determine a general rule for choosing the best place to stand if one seeks to minimize the average distance from the waiting spot to the arriving elevator.

  1. First, formulate a plan in writing on how to conduct the experiment in terms of what combinations to try and what data to record.
  2. Then, report the data you collected in a clear and concise manner.
  3. Finally, analyze the data and document your conclusions.

In-depth stage for the hands-on option

Determine clearly the difference between the current problem statement and the one of the basic stage. Then, repeat the same three steps.
