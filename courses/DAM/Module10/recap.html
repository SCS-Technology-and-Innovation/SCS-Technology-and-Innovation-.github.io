Recap

This module pushes us towards artificial intelligence. Instead of random inputs (c.f. Modules 1 – 4), we use real-world data to process (like we did in Modules 5, 6 & 9). This time we do not download the data, but gather it by measuring things as they happen (specifically, we time our own keystrokes while typing). Then, we build a very simple model to characterize patterns and use it to classify (i.e., assign a label to) the things that we do.
